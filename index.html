Key Features

Everything you need to report and track civic issues

Location-Based Reporting

Report issues with precise GPS location or map-based tagging for accurate identification of problem areas.

Real-Time Tracking

Track your complaint status in real-time from submission to resolution with unique complaint IDs.

Instant Notifications

Receive updates at every stage of your complaint resolution process via the portal.

Feedback System

Rate the resolution quality and provide feedback to help improve government services.

Data Analytics

Authorities can analyze complaint patterns to make data-driven decisions for better service delivery.

Secure & Transparent

Complete transparency in the grievance resolution process with secure user authentication.
